The Traveling Salesperson Problem is important because it helps with finding the most efficient way of covering multiple verticies 
which is very important when trying to minimise cost and save time. An example of TSP being used would be a delivery company using TSP could plan the shortest route to deliver 
the days packages to save time and money on gas. Furthermore, another application of TSP would bea tour guide using TSP to plan a tour that minimizes the travel 
time or cost of travel.
